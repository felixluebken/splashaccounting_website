—— About us

Hermain holds a Bachelor’s Degree in Accounting and a Master’s Degrees in Taxation from Florida International University.

He began his career in accounting at PricewaterhouseCoopers (PwC), the largest professional services network in the world. During his time at PwC, Hermain consulted and serviced multiple Fortune 500 companies on various accounting and tax matters. Hermain also has experience working with individuals and small businesses.
